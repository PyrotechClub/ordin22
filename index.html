About Ordin@trix



It all started 18 years ago...

A fusion of creativity, talent and skill is what Ordin@trix is all about. Ordin@trix has evolved through the years moving from an interschool festival at the state level to an international event with students participating from China, Muscat, Japan and other countries.

The event has witnessed gracious presence of celebrities like Mr. Amol Gupte and Miss Manju Singh apart from experts from various technical fields. The movies showcased here have been screened at the movie festivals by PVR Cinemas and CHINH India Festival.

About Pyrotech



Ordin@trix has come a long way

and so have the PYROTECHS. They are the very nuclei of Ordin@trix, who create fireworks in the field of technology. They breathe life into Ordin@trix.

PYROTECH members are an elite team of student computer enthusiasts. They have represented the school in various competitions and won laurels in events such as web designing, software development, image editing, movie making, 3-D modelling, Robotics, photography and others.

GENERAL GUIDELINES

  • ONLINE registrations will be done on a first come first serve basis.
  • No registration will be taken on the day of the event.
  • Each school must ensure that a student participates in only one event and that they register only one team per event. Only in Crypt@trix, individual and unlimited registrations will be entertained from the schools .
  • For all submission-based events, the entries must be mailed to the respective student event head.
  • Participants must report for the event between 8 AM to 8:30 AM. If a school reaches the venue late, timely information should be sent to the organisers.
  • Each school team must be accompanied by a teacher.
  • Registrations will close on Tuesday, 19 July '22 by 5 PM. (Registration link has been provided above)
  • Decision of the organizers & jury members members regarding any issue related to Ordin@trix 22.0 will be FINAL AND BINDING on all schools and participants.
  • To qualify for the overall trophy, a school must take part in a minimum of 5 events (excluding Crypt@trix).
