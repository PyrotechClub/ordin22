Anim@trix

Classes: 3-5
No of students in a team: 1
No of team allowed per school: 1
Mode: Offline

  • First 25 teams to register will be able to participate
  • Participants will have to prepare a short animation based on the topic, given on the spot, using Scratch 3.0
  • Duration of completion of project: 1.5 hours
  • Duration of scratch project: 1.5 - 2 minutes
  • A repository, containing some images and audios, will be provided, in case participants wants to use.
  • Plagiarism is unacceptable and will lead to disqualification.
  • Entries must be submitted in sb3 format.
  • File should be named as schoolname_participantname
  • Use of internet is no allowed
  • Judgement criteria:
    1. Originality
    2. Creativity
    3. Software Utilization
    4. Overall Presentation
