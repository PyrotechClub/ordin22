Crypt@trix

Classes: Open to all
Registration: with school/individual
Mode: Online

  • Crypt Hunt will span over 48 hours from Friday 29 July, 11:59 PM
  • The participants have to try & solve a maximum number of stages to win the hunt.
  • All participants must join the Discord Server for the main event.
  • Upon signing in to their account, participants will be able to view and answer the first question.
  • They must solve the standing question to be able to proceed to the next question.
  • In case of a tie, the participant who has solved the last question first, will be given the higher ground.
  • All the important information related to the event will be on the discord server.
  • The students do not need to register with the school in order to participate.
  • The results will not be added for the overall trophy.
