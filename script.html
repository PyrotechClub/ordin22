Script@trix

Classes: 9-12
No of students in a team: 1
No of team allowed per school: 1
Mode: Online

  • The participants will have to solve various problems based on Python basics, data structure, logics and algorithms
  • Platform: Hackerrank
  • Duration of the test: 2 hours
  • Date & timings: 1st August 2022, 5pm- 7pm
  • Participants are allowed to take help from the internet, but are not allowed to copy/paste the code.
  • The teams who are found to have simply copied the code would be immediately disqualified.
  • Each problem will have a predetermined score and the participants' score would depend on the
    number of successful test cases.
  • Participants will be ranked by their scores. If two or more participants achieve the same score,
    then the tie will be broken by the total time taken to submit the last solution
  • Judgement criteria:
    1. Completion of Programs
    2. Correct Execution/ Clarity
    3. Correct Output - test cases passed
    4. Time Taken
