Innovate@trix

Classes: 9-12
No of students in a team: 3-4
No of team allowed per school: 1
Mode: Prelims- Submission based ; Finals-Offline


QUALIFICATION ROUND

  • Innovate a business idea/ product/ service, providing a solution to the problem given in the prompt
  • Deliverables (5 in total)
    1. 3-D model/ UI design
    2. Posters (minimum 2)
    3. Logo
    4. Trailer/ Advertisement/ Commercial (any music can be used, but additional points for self-made audio; use of templates is strictly prohibited)
    5. Website / App (choose 1)
  • Prompts (choose 1)
    1. AI in farming
    2. Automation in hazardous jobs (like mining, etc)
    3. AI in cryptocurrency
  • The deliverables must be uploaded in a Google Drive folder and be submitted by 12 noon, 24 July 2022
  • Round 1 result declaration: 29 July 2022
  • Please note: if the audio is self-made, you will be required to send the mp3 file along with the project file of the audio for cross-checking.
  • Judgement criteria:
    1. Quality of deliverables
    2. Originality/ Creativity
    3. Business Idea
    4. Problem solving and solution

FINAL ROUND

  • Top 8 teams with the best business idea and quality of deliverables will pass onto the finals
  • The teams are required to make a presentation of 15-20 slides including the following points:
    1. Executive Summary
    2. Target Market Customer Analysis Competitors Operations
    3. Marketing Strategy
    4. SWOT Analysis
    5. Customer Analysis
    6. Financial Analysis
  • The presentation has to be given in the school (offline) in front of a panel of judges, but will have to be submitted prior to the event.
  • Each team will get 8-10 minutes to present their idea.
  • Submission: by 12 noon, 2nd August 2022
  • Judgement criteria:
    1. Creativity
    2. Presentation
    3. Viability
    4. Marketing Strategy
